About Me

I’m Michael Magsino, a multidisciplinary creative focused on graphic design, UX/UI, and creative technology. My work often blends visual polish with interactive thinking, and I’m especially drawn to projects that feel expressive, intentional, and human.

I’m interested in how design can shape atmosphere, storytelling, and emotional connection, whether that takes the form of a website, interface, campaign, or interactive experience.

  • Creative Technologies student at UC Santa Cruz
  • Focus areas: graphic design, UX/UI, web design, and sound-based experimentation
  • Interested in portfolio-building, visual systems, and thoughtful digital experiences

Creative Statement

Michael Magsino (he/him) is a multidisciplinary creative that primarily focuses on graphic design, UX/UI, and some recent projects within sound design. Much of his latest pieces have revolved around the dissection of mental health and the many ways he copes with them. Alongside that, various works in personal/school sites, web/app prototyping, and numerous graphic design works. Throughout his venture within the arts, he has worked with assorted tools such as the Adobe Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, Audition), Canva, Figma, and Framer, just to name a few.

With his recent work in a health-tech startup as the creative design lead, he was able to synthesize his skills in both design and marketing to produce graphic design work across socials, websites, and collaborative initiatives with mental health professionals during his time with the company. This granted him the opportunity to sharpen his ability to work within deadlines, lead projects, and collaborate with diverse teams.
