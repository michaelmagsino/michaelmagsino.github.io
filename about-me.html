Design Background

My creative work sits between graphic design, UX/UI, and creative technology. I have worked on social graphics, web layouts, brand systems, and interface concepts.

I am especially interested in how design can guide people without feeling flat or overly technical. A good interface should help someone move through an experience and should also carry the vibe of the work!
